About Movie Photography Being Present Reviews Contact IM PRODUCTION About BEING PRESENT. WE SEE THE MOMENTS THAT MATTER. IM Production is an Istanbul-based visual studio built on 15 years of observation, sharp precision, and clean storytelling. Our work comes down to one simple thing: being present. We are there when the crowd erupts, when a team celebrates a milestone, and when an audience connects with a shared vision. From massive music festivals and global brand launches to fine art exhibitions and institutional summits, our focus never changes. We simply capture the actual atmosphere of the room. Over the years, this approach has allowed us to collaborate with a wide range of partners from cultural networks like Red Bull and %100 Müzik, to global brands like Elidor and Grundig, as well as foundations and banks. Whether documenting the heavy energy of a festival or the quiet stillness of an art show with Nelumbo Studios, we handle every space with the same steady responsibility. You create the experience. We make sure it stays with you. THE SIGHT ‍Seeing is more than just looking; it is a learned discipline. Our focus is not on creating artificial perfection, but on recognizing you exactly as you are. We train our eyes to see the people who shift the energy whether you are lost in the raw rhythm of a festival or sharing a quiet moment at an exhibition. No staging, no forced moments. We see you, we feel the room, and we document the truth of that moment.
